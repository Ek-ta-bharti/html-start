Paperless Billing Options

It's easy to enroll, update your paperless billing email, or change your preference.

Here's how:

  • 1. Log in

    You will need a lab code and bill number. Both are located on a bill you've received.

  • 2. Choose your preferences

    Set your paperless billing status or update your email.

  • 3. Save your selection

To proceed, please enter the lab code and bill number found on your Quest Diagnostics bill.
